LCL output filter
Industry-standard filter built into modern inverters. Attenuates high-frequency harmonics before they reach the grid.
Reduces 5th/7th harmonics by ~85%, 11th/13th by ~95%
Inverter output level
Higher output = more switching activity = slightly higher harmonic injection. Effect is small with LCL filter.
